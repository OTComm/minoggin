Nouns

Summary

Nouns name people, places, things, and ideas.

Explanation

We only speak or write about people, places, things, and ideas. Everything that exists, has existed, or could conceptually exist, is named through the mysterious function of nouns.

Nouns can serve as SubjectsorObjectsinSentences, or Complements that complete the meaning of another noun or adjective.
