Summary

Verbs describe actions or states of being.

Explanation

Verbs describe actions or states of being. In terms of [[Sentence Structure]], verbs work with all the other words in a sentence opposite the subject to form a sentence's predicate, which indicates what the subject is doing or being.

Examples

  1. She {runs} quickly to catch the bus.
  2. He {is} a teacher.
  3. In spite of the their doubts, he {felt} the project would be a success
