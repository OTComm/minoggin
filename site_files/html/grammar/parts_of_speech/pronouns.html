Summary

Pronouns are words that replace nouns to reduce repetition and simplify communication.

Explanation

Pronouns are words that replace nouns to reduce repetition and simplify communication. They can refer to individual or groups of nouns, indicate possession, reflect back to the subject, or link clauses.

Examples

  1. {She} went to the store.
  2. {They} had to wait for the next flight because there was only one seat available.
  3. {She} was happy for {herself}.
  4. Hans was ready to take a stand, and this time {he} was willing.
  5. Dennis wanted {his} phone, wallet, and keys, but {they} had all long since settled on the ocean floor.
