Summary

Adjectives provide detail about nouns and pronouns.

Explanation

Adjectives perform a function on nouns. Whereas nouns perform the function of naming people, places, things, and ideas, and pronouns refer to nouns, adjectives perform the function of telling us more information about nouns and pronouns. That is, an adjective modifies a noun or pronoun to answer the questions:

  • Whose? (Possessive)
  • What kind(s)? (Descriptive)
  • Which one(s)? (Distributive and Demonstrative)
  • How many? (Quantitative)
