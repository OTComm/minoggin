Explanation

Every word in every phrase, clause, or sentence plays a specific role. Some roles are essential. Others add specificity. In Parts of Speech you will find a brief summary/definition of each part of speech, an explanded explantion, and examples that show the part of speech in use.
Of course, parts of speech on their own generally don't communicate enough information to form a communicate a complete idea. See Structuring Speech for topics that involve assembing and maniupulating the parts of speech.
