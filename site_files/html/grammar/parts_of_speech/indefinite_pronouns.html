Explanation

Indefinite pronouns refer to non-specific people, places, things, or quantities.

Examples

  1. {Someone} left their bag here.
  2. If {anyone} finds a hamster in the cafeteria, please notify me.
  3. {Some} say he knows the secret to a good life.
