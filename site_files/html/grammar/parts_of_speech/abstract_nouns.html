Summary

Abstract nouns name things that do not or would not have a physical form, like ideas, concepts, qualities, or states of being.

Explanation

Abstract nouns name things that do not or would not have a physical form, like ideas, concepts, qualities, or states of being. Most abstract nouns are common nouns, but some abstract nouns are proper nouns.

Examples

  1. {Wealth} {inequality} is the ruiner of all {regimes}.
  2. The beginning of a {revolution} existed before the start of the {Renaissance}.
  3. She dreamed of unicorns that pondered the distinction between {truth} and {knowledge}.

Grammar Tips
