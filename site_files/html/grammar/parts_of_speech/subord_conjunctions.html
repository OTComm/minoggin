Summary

Conjunctions are words used to connect clauses, sentences, words, or phrases within sentences.

Explanation

Conjunctions are words used to connect clauses, sentences, words, or phrases within sentences. They serve to coordinate or link parts of a sentence and are essential for constructing complex and compound sentences.

Examples

  1. She wants to dance {and} sing. (Coordinating)
  2. {Although} he was late, he didn't rush. (Subordinating)
  3. We went to the beach; {therefore}, we are very tanned. (Correlative)
  4. {Either} go now {or} stay forever. (Correlative)
  5. {While} I read, he cooked. (Subordinating)
  6. You can start {when} I signal you. (Subordinating)
