Summary

The definite article “the” functions as a referent to a specific noun that is known to the audience.

Explanation

There is only one definite article in English. The definite article “the” functions as a referent to a specific noun that is known to the audience. "The" precedes the noun that it refers to, indicating that the noun is unique or has been previously mentioned.

Examples

  1. I haven't heard this song since {the} last time I was biking down by Fort Snelling.
  2. {The} word "definite“ is an adjective that modifies {the} noun "article" to identify that particular word and to tell us something about it.
