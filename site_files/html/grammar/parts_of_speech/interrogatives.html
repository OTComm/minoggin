Summary

Articles indicate whether a noun is referring to something specific or something non-specific.

Explanation

Articles fall under the category of [[Determiners]]. Their function is to indicate the definiteness or indefiniteness of a noun; that is, they indicate whether a noun is referring to something specific or something non-specific. This dual function is expressed in the names of the two types of articles: [[Definite Articles]] and [[Indefinite Articles]].

Examples

  1. She ate {the} sandwich right in front of her hungry guest.
  2. The strategists discussed {an} intriguing plan of action that could all but guarantee our victory.
